  • What is College Living?

    College Living is a social networking tool to assist college students with finding roommates and a place to live.

  • How does College Living work?

    College Living uses GPS to locate the user and find local apartment units and local potential roommates.

  • What can I do?

    With College Living, you can...

    Talk to local college students with similar interests

    Browse user information and photos

    Browse available apartment units in your area

    View apartment amenities and photos
